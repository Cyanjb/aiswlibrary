Lenses and Optics

8 keywords in this category, each one tested and explained in plain language.

Anamorphic

Widescreen lenses with oval bokeh, horizontal flares, and gentle edge stretch; instant cinema.

Bokeh

Out-of-focus highlights rendered as soft glowing discs; blur as decoration.

Deep Focus

Everything from foreground to horizon stays sharp; layers of story in one frame.

Lens Flare

Light strikes the lens directly, blooming into streaks and ghosts.

Rack Focus

Focus glides from one subject to another mid-shot, steering attention like a hand on the chin.

Shallow Depth of Field

Razor-thin focus: the subject is sharp, the world melts into blur.

Telephoto Compression

Long lenses stack distance flat: backgrounds loom huge and close behind subjects.

Wide Angle Lens

Short focal length exaggerates depth and space; rooms stretch, faces distort up close.
