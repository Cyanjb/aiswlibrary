Scapes

5 keywords in this category, each one tested and explained in plain language.

Cityscape

A wide view of a city: rooftops, lights, layers of buildings stacked to the horizon.

Cloudscape

The sky as the whole scene: towering clouds, light beams, endless space.

Dreamscape

A landscape built from dream logic: floating islands, impossible skies, soft physics.

Microscape

A tiny world shot like a landscape: moss becomes forest, dew becomes lakes.

Seascape

The ocean as the subject: waves, horizon, weather, and light on water.
