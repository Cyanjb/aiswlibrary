Transitions and Editing

7 keywords in this category, each one tested and explained in plain language.

Cross Dissolve

One image melts into the next; time passing with a soft edge.

J-Cut

The next scene's sound arrives before its picture; audio leads the way in.

L-Cut

The previous scene's audio lingers over the new image; sound refuses to leave.

Match Cut

Two shots joined by matching shape, motion, or idea; the edit itself tells a story.

Slow Motion

Time stretched: motion rendered at high frame rate and played back slow, every detail savored.

Smash Cut

An abrupt jarring cut between extremes: loud to silent, calm to chaos.

Speed Ramp

Footage accelerates or brakes mid-shot: real time snapping into slow motion and back.
