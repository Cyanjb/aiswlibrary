Cracked Porcelain

What it means

Fine, delicate cracks across a smooth ceramic surface, like an antique teacup. Fragile beauty.

When to use it

Use for elegance with a hint of sadness or age. Beautiful on faces in surreal art.

Try this
"porcelain doll face with fine cracks, soft light"
