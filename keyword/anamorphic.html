Lenses and Optics

Anamorphic

What it means

Widescreen lenses with oval bokeh, horizontal flares, and gentle edge stretch; instant cinema.

When to use it

Use when footage should feel like a theatrical film rather than a video.

Try this
"anamorphic lens, horizontal blue flare across the frame"
