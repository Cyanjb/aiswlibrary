Lighting

Bioluminescence

What it means

Natural glow from living things, like glowing mushrooms, plankton, or deep-sea creatures. Magical and otherworldly.

When to use it

Use for fantasy scenes, night nature, and anything that should feel softly magical without being cartoonish.

Try this
"midnight forest floor, bioluminescent mushrooms"
