Lighting

Chiaroscuro

What it means

An old painting term for dramatic light against deep shadow. Think candlelit faces in dark rooms, Rembrandt style.

When to use it

Use for moody portraits, mystery, and drama. Not for cheerful scenes; it will darken everything.

Try this
"violinist in an empty theatre, chiaroscuro"
