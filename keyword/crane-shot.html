Camera Movements

Crane Shot

What it means

The camera sweeps vertically or in an arc from a raised arm, moving between ground level and overhead.

When to use it

Use for grand reveals, openings, and moments that deserve scale.

Try this
"crane shot rising from the crowd to reveal the city"
