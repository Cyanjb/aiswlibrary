Camera Movements

Dolly In

What it means

The camera physically glides toward the subject on a track. Unlike a zoom, the world shifts around them, pulling the viewer closer emotionally.

When to use it

Use for slow realizations, rising tension, or drawing attention to a decision on someone's face.

Try this
"slow dolly in on her face as she reads the letter"
Pairs well with

push-in · rack-focus

Works in

seedance · kling · veo · runway
