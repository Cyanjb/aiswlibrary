Dual Kawaii

What it means

Split-personality cute: one half sweet pastels, the other half dark or edgy, in a single design.

When to use it

Use for character designs that need contrast or before-and-after energy in one frame.

Try this
"character sheet, dual kawaii style, half pastel half black"
