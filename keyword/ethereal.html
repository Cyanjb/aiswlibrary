Ethereal

What it means

Light, airy, and not quite of this world. Glow, softness, and a floating feeling.

When to use it

Use to lift any scene toward the magical. One of the strongest single-word mood shifters.

Try this
"ethereal portrait, soft glow, floating fabric"
