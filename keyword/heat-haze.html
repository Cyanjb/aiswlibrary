Weather

Heat Haze

What it means

The shimmery, wavy air above hot roads and deserts. You can feel the temperature.

When to use it

Use for deserts, summer roads, and tension. It adds motion to still scenes.

Try this
"empty desert highway, heat haze on the horizon"
