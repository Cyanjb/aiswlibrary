Photography Styles

Macro Photography

What it means

Extreme close-up photography where tiny things fill the frame: insect eyes, dew drops, fabric weave.

When to use it

Use for detail, texture, and wonder. Backgrounds melt into soft blur automatically.

Try this
"macro photography of frost on a leaf"
Pairs well with

bokeh · shallow depth of field · microscape

Works in
