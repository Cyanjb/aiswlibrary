Lighting

Rim Light

What it means

A thin outline of light around your subject's edges, like they're glowing from behind. Separates the subject from the background beautifully.

When to use it

Use when your subject blends into the background, or you want a cinematic, polished feel.

Try this
"black cat on a fence, rim light, dusk"
