Camera Movements

Tracking Shot

What it means

The camera travels alongside a moving subject, keeping pace like a companion.

When to use it

Use for walks, chases, and any journey the viewer should take with the character.

Try this
"tracking shot following her down the market street"
Pairs well with

steadicam · handheld

Works in
