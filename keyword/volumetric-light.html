Lighting

Volumetric Light

What it means

Light you can see traveling through the air, like sunbeams through dust or fog. Adds instant atmosphere and depth.

When to use it

Use when a scene feels empty; visible light rays fill space beautifully. Forests, churches, and windows love this.

"old library, volumetric light through tall windows"
